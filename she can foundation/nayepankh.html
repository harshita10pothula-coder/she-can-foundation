Who We Are

Building brighter futures, together

NayePankh Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to transforming lives through education, vocational training, and community-led initiatives. We believe every individual deserves the chance to soar.

Since our founding, we've worked directly with local communities to identify needs and deliver sustainable programs that create lasting change.

Our Programs

Everything we do is designed to create opportunity and empower communities.

📚

Education for All

Scholarships, school supplies, and after-school tutoring for children from low-income families.

🛠️

Skill Development

Vocational training in digital literacy, tailoring, handicrafts, and entrepreneurship.

👩‍⚕️

Health & Wellness

Free health check-up camps, mental health awareness sessions, and nutrition drives.

🌱

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups, leadership training, and financial literacy workshops for women.

🌍

Environment

Tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, and awareness programs on sustainability.

🤝

Community Outreach

Food distribution, disaster relief, and support for marginalized families.
